## 13. Correction Response Letter

### What It Is
A formal letter responding to municipal staff comments or incomplete application notices, addressing each comment point-by-point and describing what revisions were made.

### Why It Matters
- Most applications receive at least one round of comments from municipal staff
- A clear, organized response speeds up approval
- Demonstrates professionalism and thoroughness

### How to Build It

```
CORRECTION RESPONSE LETTER

[Date]

[Planner Name]
[Department]
[Municipality]

RE: Response to Staff Comments
    [Application Type and File Number]
    [Property Address]

Dear [Name],

Thank you for your comments dated [date] regarding the
above-noted application. Please find our responses below.

RESPONSES TO COMMENTS:

| # | Staff Comment | Response | Drawing/Document Revised |
|---|--------------|----------|--------------------------|
| 1 | [Quote comment] | [Explain how addressed] | [List revised documents] |
| 2 | [Quote comment] | [Explain how addressed] | [List revised documents] |
| 3 | [Quote comment] | [Explain why not changed, with justification] | N/A |

REVISED MATERIALS ENCLOSED:
- [ ] Revised site plan (dated [date])
- [ ] Revised planning rationale (Section X updated)
- [ ] [Other revised documents]

Please do not hesitate to contact us with any questions.

Sincerely,
[Name]
```
