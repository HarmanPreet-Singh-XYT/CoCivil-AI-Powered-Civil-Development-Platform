## 18. Timeline and Fee Estimator

### What It Is
A realistic estimate of how long the entire development process will take and how much it will cost in government fees and professional consultant fees.

### Why It Matters
- Users consistently underestimate timelines and costs
- Helps with project financing and scheduling
- Different municipalities have different processing times

### Governing Law
- **Planning Act, s. 22(7.0.1) and 34(10.7)** — Processing timelines (OPA: 210 days, ZBA: 150 days, Subdivision: 180 days before applicant can appeal for non-decision)
- **Planning Act, s. 45** — Committee of Adjustment timelines
- **O. Reg. 549/06** — Fees municipalities can charge
- **Municipal fee by-laws** — Specific fee schedules
