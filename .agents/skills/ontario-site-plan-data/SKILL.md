## 11. Site Plan Data Summary

### What It Is
A structured summary of all site plan data — lot coverage, setbacks, grading, servicing, landscaping, parking layout, loading, garbage storage, and circulation — typically required for Site Plan Approval applications.

### Why It Matters
- **Site Plan Approval** is required under Planning Act s. 41 for most development except single-detached, semi-detached, and duplexes (note: Bill 23 exempted developments up to 10 residential units from site plan control)
- Even where site plan approval is not required, the data is needed for building permits

### Governing Law
- **Planning Act, s. 41** — Site plan control
- **Bill 23 amendments** — Exemptions for ≤10 residential units
- **Municipal site plan control by-laws and guidelines**
- **Ontario Building Code** — Grading, drainage, fire access
