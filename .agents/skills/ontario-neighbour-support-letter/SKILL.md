## 23. Neighbour Support Letter Template

### What It Is
A template letter for the applicant to provide to neighbours, explaining the proposed development and requesting their written support.

### Why It Matters
- Committee of Adjustment considers **community input** — letters of support are powerful
- Proactive engagement reduces the risk of objections at the hearing
- Some municipalities weigh neighbour support heavily in marginal cases
- Even one letter of opposition can complicate an otherwise straightforward application

### Governing Law
- **Planning Act, s. 45(5-7)** — Notice and hearing provisions
- No statutory requirement for support letters, but widely used in practice

### How to Build It

```
NEIGHBOUR SUPPORT LETTER TEMPLATE

Two components:
A) Information package TO the neighbour
B) Support letter template FOR the neighbour to sign

--- COMPONENT A: INFORMATION PACKAGE ---

[Date]

Dear Neighbour,

My name is [Applicant Name] and I am the owner of [Property
Address]. I am writing to let you know about a proposed
[renovation / addition / new construction] at my property
and to ask for your support.

WHAT I AM PROPOSING:
[2-3 sentences describing the project in plain language]
- [Key detail 1 — e.g., "Adding a second storey to our home"]
- [Key detail 2 — e.g., "The addition will be set back from
  the rear lot line by 5 metres"]
- [Key detail 3 — e.g., "No changes to the front of the house"]

WHY I NEED APPROVAL:
The proposed [project] requires [X minor variances / 
approval] from the Committee of Adjustment because 
[brief, plain language explanation — e.g., "the addition 
is slightly taller than what the zoning by-law allows"].

HOW THIS AFFECTS YOU:
I have carefully designed the project to minimize impact
on neighbouring properties:
- [Specific mitigation 1 — e.g., "No windows facing your
  property on the side wall"]
- [Specific mitigation 2 — e.g., "Shadow studies show the
  addition will not cast shadow on your backyard during
  daytime hours"]
- [Specific mitigation 3 — e.g., "We are preserving the
  mature trees along the shared property line"]

NEXT STEPS:
I have applied to the [Municipality] Committee of Adjustment.
The hearing is scheduled for [date] at [time/location].
You will receive an official notice from the City.

If you are comfortable supporting this project, I would be
grateful if you could sign the enclosed letter of support.
You may also attend the hearing to express your views.

I am happy to discuss the project with you at any time.
Please feel free to contact me at [phone] or [email].

Thank you for your consideration.

Sincerely,
[Applicant Name]
[Property Address]

[ATTACH: Sketch or simple drawing of the proposal]

--- COMPONENT B: SUPPORT LETTER TEMPLATE ---

[Date]

Committee of Adjustment
[Municipality Address]

RE: Application for Minor Variance
    File No.: [File Number]
    Property: [Subject Property Address]

Dear Members of the Committee,

I am the owner/resident of [Neighbour's Address], which
is [located adjacent to / across the street from / near]
the subject property.

I have reviewed the proposed [project description] at
[Subject Property Address] and I am writing to express
my support for this application.

[OPTIONAL — choose applicable sentences:]
□ I have reviewed the plans and believe the proposal is
  compatible with our neighbourhood.
□ The proposed changes will improve the property and 
  the streetscape.
□ I am satisfied that the project will not negatively 
  impact my property.
□ I have discussed the project with the applicant and
  am comfortable with the design.
□ The proposal is consistent with other improvements
  that have been made on our street.

I respectfully request that the Committee approve this
application.

Sincerely,

_________________________
[Neighbour Name — PRINTED]

_________________________
[Neighbour Signature]

_________________________
[Neighbour Address]

_________________________
[Date]
```
